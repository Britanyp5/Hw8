Story Description

My interactive story takes place on a soccer field in flushing. The main character has a soccer game that is about to end. It’s focused on two of the best players trying to score in the last few minutes The storyline will have choices that lead up to if they win or don’t win. The user’s choices will reflect how the story changes. The main character will have to choose to pass the ball or try and take the shots themself. These choices will have different endings.
